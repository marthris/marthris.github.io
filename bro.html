BIFROST BRIDGE

UCSB, summer semester 2015
Whole class, 15 people
Delivery in the class 3D instalations, duration 8 weeks

The bridge was made as a final project in the class 3D Intersection, and installed across the school bike lane for a period of time. The bringe was called Bifrost inspired by norrøn mythology to be the gate between the gods and the humans. Bifrost is said to be inspired by the northen light and belived to connect the living to the death.
