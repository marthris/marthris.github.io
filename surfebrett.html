CARDBOARD SURFBOARD

AHO, spring semester 2017
Individual work
Self-initiated project, duration 8 weeks

Typically, the surfboard core is made of a styrofoam blank that is manually shaped. Styrofoam is easy to shape and very cheap. In this project laser cut cardboard core is used instead. I found it interesting to get experience with how the structure of the cardboard could mimic the material abilities of styrofoam to be a more environmental option to Styrofoam. The files for the laser cutting are downloaded from Sheldrake.net who has made it possible for anyone to build a surfboard without having any knowledge about surfboard shaping.
