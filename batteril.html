Fully Automated Battery Charger

The battery charger is made as the final project in the class "Machine Design and Mechatronics". The charger takes advantage of the custom made charging magazine, which charges the battery and stopes the batteries from stacking. The batteries get sorted into AA and AAA before falling into the magazine. When the batteries are fully charged they are ejected into the "ready for use" draw.
