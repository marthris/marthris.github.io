3D PRINTED PENS

AHO, spring semester 2017
Individual work
Part of delivery in 3D visual analysis course in Solid Works, duration 2 weeks

The two pens look the same at first glance, but on closer inspection one should be able to see the differences. Through this task I would test the use of negative and positive space. The pattern that emerges when removing the cork was designed to explore the modeling of the pattern.
